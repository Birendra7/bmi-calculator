BMI Information

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that uses your height and weight to estimate if you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight for a given height.

BMI Categories

Category BMI Range (kg/m2)
Severe Thinness< 16
Moderate Thinness16 - 17
Mild Thinness17 - 18.5
Normal18.5 - 24.9
Overweight25 - 29.9
Obese Class I30 - 34.9
Obese Class II35 - 39.9
Obese Class III> 40

Additional Information

  • BMI Prime: Ratio of your BMI to the upper limit of the normal BMI range (24.9).
  • Ponderal Index: A measure of leanness that adjusts for height, used to assess health risk.
